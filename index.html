Our Programs

Age-wise learning journeys for every stage

Thoughtfully designed early years programs for ages 2 to 6.

Ages 2–3

Playgroup

Gentle transition into group learning with sensory play, movement, music, and social comfort.

  • Rhymes & rhythm
  • Sensory fun
  • Motor skills
Ages 3–4

Nursery

Language building, early numeracy, expression, and confidence through hands-on activities.

  • Story time
  • Number readiness
  • Art & expression
Ages 4–5

Pre-KG

Stronger communication, observation, writing readiness, and active classroom participation.

  • Phonics basics
  • Creative thinking
  • Confidence building
Ages 5–6

Kindergarten

A complete readiness program for formal school with literacy, numeracy, routine, and teamwork.

  • Reading readiness
  • Foundational maths
  • Social skills
